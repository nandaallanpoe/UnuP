Program Overview

The English Education program at UNU Purwokerto prepares students to become professional English language educators through comprehensive training in language pedagogy, linguistics, and cultural studies. Our program combines theoretical knowledge with practical teaching experience.

Duration

4 Years (8 Semesters)

Degree

Career Prospects

School Teacher

Teach English at primary, secondary, or high schools with opportunities for career advancement to senior teaching positions

Language Centers

Work at private language institutions, universities, or establish your own language training center

Content Developer

Create educational materials, design curricula, or work with educational publishers to develop textbooks and learning resources

International Opportunities

Teach English abroad or work with international educational organizations and exchange programs

Online Education

Work as an online English tutor, create digital learning content, or develop e-learning platforms

Corporate Training

Provide English language training for businesses, conduct workshops, and develop corporate language programs

Academic Writing

Work as an editor, proofreader, or academic writing consultant for international publications and research papers

Translation & Interpretation

Provide translation services, work as an interpreter, or assist in cross-cultural communication projects

Further Education

Pursue graduate studies in TESOL, Applied Linguistics, or Education to advance into research and higher education roles
