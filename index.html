About Our University

Universitas Nahdlatul Ulama Purwokerto, abbreviated as UNU Purwokerto, is a private university located in Purwokerto, Central Java Province, Indonesia. This university is an institution under the auspices of the Executive Board of Nahdlatul Ulama and was established on November 30, 2016

Our Vision

To become a leading university that excels in education, research, and community service based on Ahlussunnah wal Jamaah An-Nahdliyah values.

Our Mission

  • Organizing quality higher education based on Ahlussunnah wal Jamaah An-Nahdliyah values
  • Conducting research that contributes to the development of science and technology
  • Implementing community service programs that benefit society
  • Building strong partnerships with various stakeholders
